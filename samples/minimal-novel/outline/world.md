# World

Three realms float above a dying ocean.  Cultivators ascend by absorbing
the world's residual memory; mortals trade stories for protection.

## Factions

- **Azure Order** — scholar-monks who preserve the canon.
- **Iron Court** — warlords who weaponize memory into banners.
- **The Hush** — outcasts who remember what was paid to be forgotten.

## Tone

Melancholic wuxia with a slow-burn sense of inevitability.
